Who We Are.

We are a dedicated team committed to transforming the educational landscape in Mozambique. Our mission is to ensure that every child in the country has access to a safe and dignified learning environment, enabling them to receive a quality education and reach their full potential.

Our Story.

Our journey began with a critical need identified in the primary school of Boboli, where many children were forced to study under trees due to the lack of classrooms. With the support of friends and our own resources, we initiated the construction of new classrooms to provide these children with the learning environment they deserve.

Our mission.

Our mission is to build and equip classrooms, train teachers, and provide necessary educational resources across Mozambique. We believe that every child deserves a safe, comfortable, and inspiring place to learn, regardless of their background or location.

Our vision.

Our vision is to create a future where every child in Mozambique can learn in a secure and supportive environment, free from the challenges of studying outdoors. By improving educational infrastructure and resources, we aim to foster a generation of well-educated, empowered individuals who can contribute positively to their communities and the country as a whole.

Why Support Us

By supporting our initiative, you are investing in the future of Mozambique’s children. Your contributions help us build more classrooms, equip schools with essential resources, and ensure that every child has the opportunity to thrive academically.

Together, we can make a significant impact and create lasting change in the lives of thousands of children.

Join us in our mission to transform education in Mozambique and provide a brighter future for all.
