School Network Building the Future.

In Mozambique, many children are forced to receive their education under trees, sitting on the bare ground due to a lack of classrooms. This situation is dire and demands immediate action. At School Network, we are committed to building classrooms for these children, but we can't do it alone, we need your help. By joining our network and contributing to this vital cause, you can help support the future architects of our world. Together, let’s take a step toward a brighter future

Our Goal

Our overall goal is to ensure that all children in Mozambique have access to a safe and dignified learning environment, providing a quality education that allows for the full development of their academic and personal abilities. We want to build suitable classrooms, equip schools with the necessary resources, and thereby transform education in the country, creating opportunities for a more promising future for all Mozambican children.

Learn More

Where we are

With our own funds and the support of our friends, we start the construction of 3 new classrooms for the primary school in Boboli. This essential project aims to replace outdoor classes while providing a safe and suitable environment for all classes. We are already moving forward with the construction of 7 classrooms, significantly improving children's learning conditions and positively impacting their academic performance.
